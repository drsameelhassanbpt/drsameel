Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our physiotherapy services and telerehabilitation programs.

I specialize in treating a wide range of conditions including back and neck pain, sports injuries, post-surgical rehabilitation, arthritis, joint mobility issues, muscle strains, and chronic pain conditions. Each treatment plan is customized to address your specific needs and recovery goals.

Research shows that telerehabilitation can be equally effective as in-person treatment for many conditions. With high-definition video technology, real-time movement analysis, and personalized exercise programs, patients often achieve similar or better outcomes due to increased convenience and adherence to treatment plans.

You'll need a device with a camera and microphone (smartphone, tablet, or computer), stable internet connection, and adequate space for movement. Basic equipment like resistance bands or light weights may be recommended based on your treatment plan, but many exercises can be performed using household items.

You can book appointments through our online booking system, call our clinic directly, or use our mobile app. We offer flexible scheduling including evening and weekend slots for telerehabilitation sessions. Initial consultations are available both in-person and virtually.

Treatment duration varies depending on your condition, severity, and individual response to therapy. Acute injuries may require 4-6 weeks, while chronic conditions might need 8-12 weeks or longer. We continuously monitor your progress and adjust the treatment plan to ensure optimal recovery outcomes.

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans and can help you understand your coverage benefits. We also offer direct billing services for eligible plans and flexible payment options for those without insurance coverage. Contact our office to verify your specific insurance benefits.
